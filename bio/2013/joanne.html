Dr. Joanne Cote-Bonanno, Bio

Dr. Joanne Cote-Bonanno, Associate Provost for Academic Programs and Assessment, Montclair State University

Dr. Joanne Cote-Bonanno, Ph.D. is the Associate Provost for Academic Programs and Assessment at Montclair State University in New Jersey. She has been in Higher Education for over 30 years and has served as a faculty member, and Director of grant projects at Montclair State. She also served as Dean of Adult and Professional Programs, Dean of Curriculum and Faculty Development and Director of Online Learning at Centenary College.

In her current position at Montclair State University she is responsible for overseeing the Student Learning Outcomes Assessment processes and co-chairs the Committee on University Effectiveness. In addition, she manages all Curriculum and Accreditation procedures, including the Online Program curriculum.

She has a Ph.D. from Seton Hall University and an MA and BA from Montclair State University.
