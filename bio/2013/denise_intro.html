Denise O'Shea Bio

Denise O'Shea, Head of Access Services & Systems, Montclair State University

In her role as the Head of the Access Services and Systems at MSU's
Harry A. Sprague Library, Denise oversees the Circulation Department,
manages the library catalog, various social media sites and other
technologies. In addition to her library duties, she is currently
pursuing a M.A. in Public and Organizational Relations also at
Montclair State University. Her library background includes working
in Public, Special, Academic and Health/Science libraries. She also
has 10 years of experience working in the tech industry. She is the
2012-13 President of the New Jersey Library Association's College
and University Section / ACRL-NJ and serves on a number of
statewide professional committees.
