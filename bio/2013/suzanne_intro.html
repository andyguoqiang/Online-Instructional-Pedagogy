Dr. Suzanne McCotter Bio

Dr. Suzanne McCotter, Chairperson, Counseling and Educational Leadership, Montclair State University

Dr. Suzanne Schwarz McCotter is Department Chair and Associate Professor of Counseling and Educational Leadership at Montclair State University. She received her Ph.D. in Middle School Education from The University of Georgia. A recipient of a Spencer Foundation Grant to examine the ways that school leaders learn to use data, her other scholarly interests include instructional supervision, reflection in the practice of teachers and leaders, and the work of middle school principals.
