Rubrics


Rubrics not only provide grading guidelines, but also explain what students are supposed to do in order to complete assignments. When grading learning performance or a process, a formal set of criteria can be used to provide more structure and reliability . This set of criteria is often called a rubric.

  • List characteristics that describe the performance
  • Apply a fixed scale that rates the quality of a product
  • Communicate expectations of high quality performance to students
  • Help teachers to be more accurate, unbiased and consistent with grading
  • Can be used by students, peers and instructors

A rubric can be used, not only as grading criteria for assessments, but also as guidelines for students on how to approach assignments/projects. Rcampus is a great online tool to create grading rubrics. Some sample grading rubrics are listed below:
