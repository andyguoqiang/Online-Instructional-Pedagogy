Summer Institute 2013

The 2013 Technology Training and Integration (TTI) Summer Institute will take place June 17th -19th. This three day event provides a broad range of perspectives on online and hybrid course design and teaching. Participants will learn about online program development at MSU and hear from faculty presenters on their experience teaching online courses including strategies that promote active learning, facilitate collaboration, and utilize effective online learning assessments. There will be opportunities to participate in relevant technology workshops focused on effective online instruction, interaction and assessment, as well as dedicated time to work on designing or improving an online or hybrid course with consultation provided by our staff and faculty peers. âœ“ Register now for the TTI Summer Institute 2013!

Take a look at our tentative agenda. You may also see the 2012 Summer Institute resources, agenda and presentations, here.

Who Should Attend?

Montclair State University faculty who:

  • Plan on or are interested in teaching hybrid/online courses
  • Currently teach hybrid/online courses
  • Seek to integrate effective technologies into online teaching

Institute participants should have experience working with Blackboard 9 prior to the event. If you are not familiar with Blackboard we highly recommend that you to attend one of our Introduction to Blackboard training courses prior to the Summer Institute.
