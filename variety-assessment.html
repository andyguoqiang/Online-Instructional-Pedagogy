Use a Variety of Assessment Types

In assessing online learning, it is important to create a “mix” of assessments that cover the multiple dimensions of learning that online courses can employ. Traditional tests become a smaller part of the grade as you move towards encouraging student interaction on group projects and other activities.

Different types of assessments include:

  • Tests, Exams, Quizzes
  • Pre & Post Testing
  • Project Reports
  • Peer Reviews
  • Role Playing
  • Research Papers
  • Reflective Writing
  • Presentations
  • Discussion
  • Surveys, interviews and observations
  • Checklists
  • Group discussion
  • Case studies
  • Journals
  • Reading responses
