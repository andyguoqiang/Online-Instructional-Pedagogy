Incorporate ePortfolios

An ePorfolio is a collection of digital files (artifacts) that are shared electronically for the purpose of reflection, comment, and evaluation. Students may use Porfolios to collect evidence for summative assessment, and to demonstrate achievement and can be very valuable for students to showcase their work beyond the classroom.
