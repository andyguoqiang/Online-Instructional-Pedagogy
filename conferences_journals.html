Conferences & Journals

There are several conferences and journals in the field of Instructional Design and Technology which may be of interest to you as you consider the design and delivery of your own online courses. You may also consider presenting your work at these selected conferences or publishing your research and findings in IDT journals listed below.

Conferences:

  • SLOAN-C (The Sloan Consortium): http://sloanconsortium.org/
  • AECT (Association for Educational Communications and Technology): http://aect.site-ym.com/
  • EDUCAUSE: http://www.educause.edu/
  • ELD: http://eld.montclair.edu/
  • ISTE (International Society for Technology in Education): http://www.iste.org/welcome.aspx
  • AERA (American Educational Research Association): http://www.aera.net/
  • SITE (Society for Information Technology & Teacher Education): http://site.aace.org/
  • ICLS (The International Conference of the Learning Sciences): http://www.isls.org/icls.html
